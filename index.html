About us

We are a small lovable pizza shop. Located in River Falls, Wi,. The business started in 1982 when Bob Boberson started Luigi's in college. For years Luigi's was comfortable spot to eat and hangout for families and college students alike. One day in 1996 a delivery driver by the name of Charles "Chuck" Kamrowski would purchase the spot with hand shake and a smile. Over the next 26 years Chuck would raise a family at Luigi's and grow the size of Luigi's many pizza loving patrons.
